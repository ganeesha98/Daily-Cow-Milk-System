Dairy Cow Milking System

Background

Humans have been milking cows for more than 8,000 years. Although dairy processing and dairy storage has seen significant technological improvement, many parts of the milking procedure remain cumbersome, labor-intensive, and resource inefficient.

Mission

RoBovine seeks to improve the welfare of dairy farm owners, workers, and animals by making the milking process more productive, efficient, hygienic, and environmentally friendly.

Product

RoBovine has been designing and field-testing for the last two years and now has a production-ready design. The RoBovine system will revolutionize the milking process by increasing farm productivity, lowering milking costs, improving cow comfort and stress levels, and reducing environmental impact.

Making the world a better place

Humans have been milking cows for over 8,000 years.

Dairy packs a serious nutrient punch, effectively, efficiently and affordably providing the annual protein requirements of 169 million people and the annual calcium requirements of over three-quarters of the population.

One cow at a time

We think it's about time to make the milking process more productive for farmers, more comfortable for cows, and more environmentally friendly for the planet.

Good & Clean Milking Process

Wash and dry dirty teats before milking. Only milk animals with clean, dry teats. Check the udder and teats for any abnormalities which may indicate clinical mastitis. The foremilk may be extracted and checked for abnormalities before each animal is milked.
